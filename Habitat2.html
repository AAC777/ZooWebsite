Badger

https://www.desertusa.com/animals/images/badger.jpg

Badgers are pretty large, robust, short legged, and thick and bushy animals. Badgers measure from 1 1/2 to 2 feet in length, with their tail adding another 4 to 6 inches. Badgers may weigh from 6 to 25 pounds. They have a thick shaggy coat, triangular shaped head with powerful jaws. Badgers are carnivores and eat small rodents, reptiles, birds, rats, and mice.

Tocos are the most recognizable member of the toucan family Their most recognizable trait is their long. orange and yellow bill.Tocos can grow up to 1.5 to 2 feet in height. They can weigh from 1 to 2 pounds. Tocos are omnivores like humans so they can eat vegetation and meat. They eat oranges, lizards, insects, and eggs.
