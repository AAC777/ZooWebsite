Bengal Tiger

https://www.rainforestanimals.net/wp-content/uploads/2018/12/bengaltigersmall.jpg

Bengal Tigers are large cats with dark orange color and black stripes. They have large paws, a long curled tail, and are more slender in appearance than their tiger cousins. Their diets consist of antelopes, boars, monkeys, pigs, and birds. The Bengal Tigers can also weigh up 400-600 pounds.

Toco Tucan

https://www.rainforestanimals.net/wp-content/uploads/2019/01/tocotoucan.jpg

Tocos are the most recognizable member of the toucan family. Their most recognizable trait is their long, orange and yellow bill. Tocos can grow up to 1.5 to 2 feet in height. They can weigh from 1 to 2 pounds. Tocos are omnivores like humans so, they can eat vegetation and meat. They eat oranges, lizards, insects, and eggs.
